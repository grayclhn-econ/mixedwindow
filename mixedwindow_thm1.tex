If Assumptions~\ref{a1} -- \ref{a4} hold then
$\sqrt{P} (\fb - \E \fb(\btrue)) \to^d N(0, \Sigma)$,
with
\begin{align*}
    \Sigma &= S_{ff} + S_{fg} + S_{fg}' + 2 \, S_{gg}, &
    S_{ff}  &= \lim P \var(\fb(\btrue)), \\
    S_{fg}  &= \lim P \cov(\fb(\btrue), \gb(\btrue)), &
    S_{gg}  &= \lim P \var(\gb(\btrue)).
\end{align*}
